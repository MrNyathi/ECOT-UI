Apply online with Eswatini College of Technology

Ensuring your application is complete with all the required info and documents included will allow us to quickly process your application.

Step 1:

Ensure you have the following information on hand:

  • Personal Information, Next of Kin information
  • Education history
  • Your Results

Step 2:

Scan and save the following documents ahead of time (pdf or jpeg):

  • Certified copy of your ID, Next of Kin
  • Certified copies of all academic qualifications
  • Certified copy of your statement of results

Step 3:

Click Create Application button and complete the registration form in the application portal.

Step 4:

Login and follow the on-screen prompts to complete your online application. If, at any stage, you need help contact the school.
